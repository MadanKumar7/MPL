Auction

Rule 1

A player who has played in one team is not allowed to play in another team.

Rule 2

This tournament would be played with Wilson 4 Tennis ball.

Rule 3

Each Match would start with a new ball. (In case of ball damage/loss old ball will be provided.)

Rule 4

League matches would be of 6 overs ( 2,2,1,1) (In case of time shortage overs will be reduced accordingly.)

Rule 5

QF, SF and Final will be of 7 overs (3,2,1,1) (In case of time shortage overs will be reduced accordingly.)

Rule 6

LBW and Leg byes are not applicable for this tournament. Batsman cannot be ruled out in any form for leg byes and no run would be counted.

Rule 7

One bouncer is allowed per over, provided the ball passes between the shoulder and the head. If the ball passes over the head, it will be declared as a wide.

Rule 8

Free hit is allowed only for crease No ball.

Rule 9

Round arm throw Bowling is allowed.

Rule 10

On-field umpire’s decision would be the final one.

Rule 11

In case of draw, points will be divided in league matches.

Rule 12

In case of draw, super over will be played in qualifier round. Chasing team will bat first. (In case of super over draw bowl out will be played.)
